About Us

"Nature heals everything"

Medicinal plants are very useful plant used for herbal medicine, which can boost the immune system. The back-to-nature lifestyle and the high cost of modern medicines have made the demand for medicinal plants even higher, not only in Indonesia but also in the world.

You find interesting plant but don't know its name? Wanna know how to make herb medicine? Or you are curious about the health benefit of a plant? Ask Herbify! It holds all of information about medicinal plant.

Herbify is an android mobile application used to identify medicinal plants by artificial intelligence technology and provides information about its health benefit and herbal medicine recipes. A must-have app for herbalist, researcher, and nature lovers!
